Diseases - like heart disease and stroke* are the top leading causes of death in the world. Are you safe from them?

[*]http://www.who.int/gho/mortality_burden_disease/causes_death/top_10/en/



Probably. Doctors and researchers have pointed out that lifestyle and environment significantly influence the likelihood of many diseases.

However, what we may not always be aware of is the co-occurrence of many diseases and the evolution of ailments bundles with age. We know that high blood pressure is likely to occur often in patients who suffered a stroke. Is there an association between mental health and digestive disorders? What else?
